Welcome to BAL Robotics

The leading innovator on Artificially Intelligent Robots. Our mission is to provide the highest quality healthcare robots on the market in order to facilitate patients and revolutionize the medical industry.

BAL Robotics, headquartered in Sammamish Washington, was founded in 2017 by 3 young entrepreneurs with one goal: to make healthcare more efficient. We have since grown from a garage startup to one of the hottest tech companies on the west coast, employing over 500 engineers, computer scientists, data scientists, and more.
