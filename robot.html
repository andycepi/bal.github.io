Our Robot

Healthbot

Our flagship robot, the Healthbot, is a capable medical assistant with the ability to communicate fluently with patients in different languages, diagnose patients and provide routine check-ups, administer medicine, and more.

A full list of its features are:

  • A friendly appearance to sooth patients
  • A human-like simulated personality
  • Translation to hundreds of different languages using built in real-time translators
  • Medical assistance
  • Ability to diagnose patients using machine learning, similar to IBM Watson
  • Cameras and sensors to recognize and assess patients
  • A monitor to display patient metrics, such as height, weight, blood pressure, heart rate, and more
  • Basic medical care, including check-ups, prescriptions, and administration of medicine
  • In home care for patients who need constant surveillance
